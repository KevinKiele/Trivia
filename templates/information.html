Rules


Welcome to Locked Up!

Locked up is a game where you need to answer trivia questions. The rules are simple:


- There is a singleplayer mode and a multiplayer mode. You can choose if you want to play alone or against other players to test out your intelligence!



- The questions are divided in three categories: general, sport and history.



- You have to choose your answer from four buttons. Three buttons have the wrong answer and one button has the CORRECT answer.



- Every CORRECT answer rewards the player with 1 point. After a game the score will show up and in multiplayer the score will be compared. The player with the highest score WINS!



- Your score will add up after every game and is shown on your profile.
