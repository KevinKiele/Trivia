Welcome to Fenway Park


Fenway Park is a baseball park located in Boston, Massachusetts at 4 Yawkey Way near Kenmore Square.
Since 1912, it has been the home for the Boston Red Sox, the city's American League baseball team.
It is the oldest ballpark in MLB.
